Detailed Description

template<typename alphabet_type, typename alphabet_extension_type, typename size_type>
struct Validate4< alphabet_type, alphabet_extension_type, size_type >::BktInfo

record L-type, S-type and LMS bucket sizes (no matter empty or non-empty) in SA & LCP

Elements in SA & LCP are naturally divided into multiple buckets and each bucket contains all the suffixes starting with an identical character. Each bucket can be further divided into two parts, whhere the left and right part separately contain the L-type and S-type suffixes, respectively. For description convenience, we denote the two part as L-type bucket and S-type bucket, respectively.
